Selected Highlights

  • NeuroGaze‑Distill
    • Teacher prototypes from DREAMER & MAHNOB‑HCI EEG topomaps; consolidated 5×5 Valence/Arousal grid.
    • Student trained on FERPlus/AffectNet‑mini; no EEG or gaze needed at deployment.
    • Lightweight regularizers including Proto‑KD (cosine) and depression‑inspired geometric prior (D‑Geo).
  • EmoLoom‑2B
    • ~2B‑parameter LLM for emotion‑centric QA/VAD; releases on GitHub & Hugging Face.
    • VAD‑preserving losses and an appraisal verifier; stress‑tests for valence flips and format robustness.
    • Reproducible pipelines with Conda, offline caches, and deterministic evaluation scripts.
